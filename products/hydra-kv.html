Architecture

  • · Prefill node compresses KV cache with E8-lattice algorithm
  • · QUIC transport with TLS 1.3 (no plaintext KV in transit)
  • · Decode node verifies integrity via TTTPS seal on each chunk
  • · GapVec5 selects optimal transfer path (ISP latency, loss rate, throughput)
  • · CRDT deduplication eliminates redundant KV transfer across sessions

TTTPS Audit Integration

Every KV chunk transfer generates a TTTPS temporal token — cryptographically binding the transfer to a verifiable timestamp and commitment chain.
